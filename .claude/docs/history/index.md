# Project history

Durable narratives distilled from sprint folders. Each page captures a sprint's *enduring* outcomes: shipped features, walked-back ideas (which become caveats elsewhere), enduring decisions.

Sprint folders under `.claude/project/sprint-N/` remain the source of truth for the *process* — discussion, plans, boards. The pages here are the *result* — what an outsider needs to read to understand what changed and why.

Planned pages are listed in the [L-2 backlog].
