## A Semantic Reflection

I would say various narrative things. These things might be linked when I say [first-order logic]({wikipedia pahe}). And periodically I would list SRT formulas for consideration:

`x -is> symbol <|=|> Ey: x != y & x =x> y`

And then I would write more code.

It might involve multiple paragraphs. Sometimes it would use Latex

$\neg X \implies \neg Y$

But mostly just to show that I changed it in SRT:

`!x =|> !y`

And that's how a reflection would go.
