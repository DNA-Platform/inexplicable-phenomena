This repository is a **public library**, and **philosophy** is one of its subjects. A subject is known only through its **books** — and a book is where a subject's data is catalogued: what the subject holds, how it's organized, and how a reader moves through it. Today **philosophy** is a folder with no books, so there is nothing yet to read.

Write **philosophy**'s books — the ones that express how its data is catalogued, so the subject can be read the way any book in the library is.

Done when: **philosophy** has books that express how its data is catalogued.
